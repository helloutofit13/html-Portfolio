The Best Movies According to Harpreet

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time___

1.Yes I am Student

Set in India, it shows the life and struggles of an International student in Canada.

2.Aaja Mexico Challiye

Pamma is an ambitious youngster from Punjab who wants to go abroad. But this one dream turns his life around as he becomes caught in dangerous situations. Will Pamma be able to make it out of the jungles of Mexico?

3. Main Hoon Na

An army major goes undercover as a college student. His mission is both professional and personal: to protect his general's daughter from a radical militant, and to find his estranged half-brother.
